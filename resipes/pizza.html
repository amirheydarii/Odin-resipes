Description

This recipe takes all the good things we love on pizza and combines them w ith egg noodles to form a great, one-dish casserole to feed your family.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

  • 1 cup sliced pepperoni sausage

  • 2 cups uncooked egg noodles

  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped

  • Â½ pound lean ground beef

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 16 ounces pizza sauce

  • 4 tablespoons milk

  • 1 onion, chopped

Steps

      Step 1 : Cook noodles according to package directions.

      Step 2 : Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

      Step 3 : In a medium skillet over medium-high heat, brown the ground beef with the onion,
      garlic and green bell pepper. Drain excess fat. Stir in the noodles,
      pepperoni, pizza sauce and milk, and mix well.
      Pour this mixture into a 2-quart casserole dish.

      Step 4 : Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20 minutes,
      top with the cheese, then bake for 5 to 10 more minutes.
